Mamma mia, here I go again!

Posted on January 8, 2019

Hi there, I’m Deniz Alp (@lambdazai). This is the very first post of my new blog; Existential Types. I’ve made numerous attempts to blog in the past and failed every single time.

I recently made a new year’s resolution to “read lots of functional pearls1” in 2019.

new year's resolution: read lots of functional pearls

— deshalb (@lambdazai) December 29, 2018

And thanks to Ken’s suggestion2 I’ve decided to pick up blogging again. While I don’t have a definitive schedule, I’m hoping to post at least once a month and not just about functional pearls; expect book reviews, Haskell and a bunch of other fun3 things.

See you next time!
